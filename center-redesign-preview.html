About

Hale Ola Wellness Retreat is a serene sanctuary nestled on the lush Hāmākua Coast of Hawaiʻi's Big Island. Founded in 2018, we offer transformative wellness experiences that blend traditional Hawaiian healing practices with modern therapeutic modalities. Our 5-acre property features meditation gardens, a natural hot spring, and dedicated treatment spaces designed to support your journey to wholeness. Whether you're seeking a weekend retreat, daily classes, or individual sessions, our team of experienced practitioners is here to guide your path to wellness.
