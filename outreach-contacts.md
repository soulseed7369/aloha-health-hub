## Contact Management Tips

1. **Personalization:** Always address editors/contacts by name when available
2. **Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday emails typically have best open rates
3. **Subject Lines:** Use specific modality names or island focus for higher engagement
4. **Follow-up:** One gentle follow-up after 2 weeks if no response
5. **Relationship Building:** Start with guest posts before requesting links
6. **Local Angle:** Always emphasize Hawaii-specific wellness ecosystem focus
7. **Compliance:** Ensure any guest posts link naturally to hawaiiwellness.net without over-optimization
