## Appendix: Detailed Scoring Methodology

Quality Score = (Avatar × 30) + (Bio × 30) + (Contact × 25) + (Modalities × 15)

Maximum: 100 points

- **Avatar (30 pts):** Profile photo present and non-placeholder
- **Bio (30 pts):** Minimum 20 characters of professional description
- **Contact (25 pts):** Full: email + phone (25); Partial: one contact method (12); None (0)
- **Modalities (15 pts):** At least one wellness modality tagged

Penalties: None (additive scoring only)

Scoring by quartile:
- 80-100: High quality, ready to promote
- 60-79: Good, minor polish needed
- 40-59: Acceptable with improvements
- 20-39: Poor, significant work required
- 0-19: Unusable without major intervention
